     - Compute `targetState`:

       - Si `live && session.process_alive`: `targetState = 'running'`.

       (`debounceStore.get(session.workspace_ref) ?? {pending_state: null, tick_count: 0}`).

       - Si `pending_state !== targetState`: reset (`{pending_state: targetState, tick_count: 1}`).

         actualiza `tab_alive`/`needs_input`/`last_seen_alive`,

     `workspace_ref` está en `liveByRef`, rescatar — mover a sessions con
