<objective>
Deliver the two `src/daemon/` modules (D-02) on top of the Wave-1 primitives. `lifecycle.js` generalizes the shipped `polling.js`/`polling-daemon.js` daemon plumbing into name-parametrized `startDaemon`/`stopDaemon`/`statusDaemon` (its real consumer is Phase 66's `kodo up`/`stop`/`status`, delivered here). `run.js` is the single foreground funnel (`runDaemon()`): it composes `startServer({managed:true})` + conditional `startPolling` into ONE process with ONE PID file (`~/.kodo/kodo.pid`), installs signal handlers first, and is the single owner of teardown and exit (D-01, D-05, D-06).

Purpose: `run.js` is the compose funnel that both `kodo up` (detached, Phase 66) and launchd/`brew services` (direct, Phase 66) will invoke; getting the single-owner teardown and conditional polling right here is what keeps `brew services` from double-forking or crash-looping. `lifecycle.js` is generic plumbing shaped for its Phase 66 consumer.
Output: `src/daemon/lifecycle.js`, `src/daemon/run.js`, and their DI unit tests.
</objective>
